PROJECT 01 · TRACKING ARCHITECTURE
GA4 & GTM Attribution & Conversion Tracking Architecture
Problem

A multi-business-model fintech platform had fragmented tracking with ~30% unattributed conversions. Marketing budget allocation decisions were based on incomplete data, leading to inefficient spend.

Approach

Designed a comprehensive GTM tagging architecture from scratch — custom event schemas, data layer implementation, cross-domain tracking, and server-side event deduplication. Built multi-touch attribution model covering paid, organic, email, and referral touchpoints.

Outcome

Attribution accuracy improved by 40%+, enabling confident budget reallocation toward highest-performing channels and a measurable ROAS improvement.

PROJECT 02 · SQL · COHORT ANALYSIS
Fintech Trading Cohort & Funnel Retention Analytics
Problem

A fintech trading platform needed visibility into where users dropped from onboarding, which acquisition cohorts had the best LTV, and what behavioral signals predicted churn.

Approach

Built scalable SQL pipelines processing multi-million-row datasets. Designed cohort profitability models segmented by acquisition channel, geography, and product. Identified the Install→KYC→first-deposit funnel stages causing the most drop-off.

Outcome

Install-to-KYC conversion increased by 17%. 30-day retention improved 12%. Time-to-first-purchase reduced by 17% through targeted onboarding interventions.

PROJECT 03 · PERFORMANCE MARKETING
Travel Platform — Paid Acquisition Scale & CAC Reduction
Problem

A travel agency needed to grow qualified bookings within a fixed budget, with no standardized approach to campaign testing, attribution, or audience segmentation.

Approach

Restructured Google Ads campaigns across Search, Display, and Performance Max. Implemented systematic creative A/B testing, lookalike audience layering, and sequential retargeting funnels. Rebuilt attribution via GTM and GA4 to isolate true converting touchpoints.

Outcome

35% growth in qualified acquisition, CAC reduced by 25%, CPO by 19%, and ROAS improved by 20% — all within the same monthly budget of $12K.

PROJECT 04 · ASO · MOBILE GROWTH
Fintech App Store Optimization & Mobile Install Growth
Problem

A fintech mobile app had high CPI from paid installs and poor organic discoverability in app stores, creating dependency on paid spend with poor unit economics.

Approach

Conducted full ASO audit — keyword research, metadata optimization, screenshot A/B testing, review response strategy, and category positioning. Integrated ASO signals with paid acquisition to lower blended CPI.

Outcome

CPI reduced by 22%, monthly install rates grew 32%, and blended CAC improved as organic installs increased share of total acquisition.

ASO Google UAC A/B Testing SQL Analytics
PROJECT 05 · DASHBOARDING · AUTOMATION
Executive Marketing Dashboard — CAC, LTV & Revenue Forecasting
Problem

CMO and leadership had no single source of truth for marketing performance. Reports were assembled manually from multiple platforms weekly, consuming 10+ hours and often containing inconsistencies.

Approach

Built an automated data pipeline from GA4, Google Ads, and Meta Ads into BigQuery, then visualized via Looker Studio. Included CAC trends by channel, LTV cohort curves, retention waterfalls, and forward-looking revenue forecasts.

Outcome

Reporting time reduced by 40%. Dashboard became the foundation for CMO-level quarterly reviews and strategic budget decisions.
